# D36 First-Cohort Instructions

Audience: 5-10 trusted early users. This is local/private review only unless Alex explicitly approves broader sharing.

## What To Open

- Open `generated/multimodal/D36/replay.html` on a phone-sized browser viewport.
- If audio is unavailable, review visual replay structure and narration text variants only.

## What To Try First

- Watch the opening screen and first two memory cards.
- Tap Cards, Route, Media, and Variants.
- Check whether the active image details match what you see.
- Read one short narration variant and one longer variant.
- If audio exists, play and seek to confirm the image still follows the audio timeline.

## Feedback Questions

1. What is your first impression?
2. What is the most confusing part?
3. What is your favorite part?
4. How is the mobile usability?
5. Would you continue watching the next day?
6. would you share it?
7. Do you have any privacy concern?
8. What is one thing to improve?
9. What is your 1-10 score?

## Privacy Note

- Do not forward the local page, generated files, screenshots, images, or audio unless explicitly approved.
- Do not include secret keys, local absolute paths, or private account details in feedback.

## Known Limitations

- This is a readiness package, not UAT PASS.
- This does not include P2 feedback operations, weekly review, AR, VR, or production deployment.
